BMW 7 Series Information

Overview

The BMW 7 Series is a luxury sedan known for its comfort, performance, and advanced features.

Models

  • BMW 740i
  • BMW 750i
  • BMW 745e (plug-in hybrid)
  • BMW M760i (high-performance)

Features

  • High-quality leather upholstery
  • Advanced infotainment system with touchscreen
  • Gesture control for various functions
  • Adaptive LED headlights
  • Driving assistance systems

Performance

The BMW 7 Series offers a range of powerful engines, providing smooth acceleration and responsive handling.

Pricing

Price Range: $86,800 - $157,800
